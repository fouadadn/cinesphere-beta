THE FOUNDATION

2019 | +16 | SCIENCE FICTION

Foundation is an American science fiction streaming television series created by David S. Goyer and Josh Friedman for Apple TV+, loosely based on the Foundation series of stories by Isaac Asimov. It features an ensemble cast led by Jared Harris, Lee Pace, Lou Llobell and Leah Harvey.[2] The series premiered on September 24, 2021. In October 2021, the series was renewed for a second season, which premiered on July 14, 2023.
